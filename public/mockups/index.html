MIBM punchcard variants

Three takes on the per-rider 31-day strip. Real data from the dev DB.

A
Punchcard · monochrome
Dark, restrained, GitHub-grid feel. Brightness encodes daily mileage.
B
Sparkbar · paper
Cream paper, vertical bars. Height encodes mileage; warm tones for long days.
C
Streak rope · pearls on a string
Each day is a bead. Consecutive ridden days are linked, making streaks visible at a glance.
