MIBM landing-page mockups

Five static reimaginings of the May is Bike Month leaderboard. Same data, very different vibes.

01
Terminal session
Green-on-black CRT, ASCII banner, calendar heatmap rendered as a cal --user command.
02
Stage map
Riders as jerseys progressing along a May 1 → May 31 route. Yellow / green / polka.
03
Newspaper broadsheet
The May Gazette, Cycling Edition. Drop caps, halftone portrait, stage weather reports.
04
Y2K CD-ROM
Win98 windows, beveled buttons, scrolling marquee, blink tag energy. Visitor counter included.
05
Punchcard heatmap
Each rider is one row of 31 cells. Brightness = miles. Daily-total ridge above.
